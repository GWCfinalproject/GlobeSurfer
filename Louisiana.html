LOUISIANA

The Pelican State

Louisiana is a small state located in the south central part of United States. Its capital of Baton Rouge is known as the hub of Louisiana containing all things culturally unique to this eccentric and fun loving state. Its largest city of New Orleans is known for its eclectic taste, and yearly mardi gras parades, full of color, clothes, and of course lots and lots of beads! Remeber when visiting this fun loving state pack lots of light airy clothing as the humidity can get uncomfortable but, don't let this damper your spirits as thats only one con among the the millions of pros. So don't waste any time, book your ticket and hotel and celebrate the rich history and kind hospitality!

Interesting Facts

Interesting Fact #1

OMG Really?!

Mamou bills itself as "The Cajun Music Capital of the World." Mamou musicians, in particular the musicians who have perform at Fred's Lounge have been a major force in expanding the audience for Cajun music far beyond Southwest Louisiana.

Interesting Fact #2

THATS AWESOME!

Louisiana has the tallest state capitol building in the United States; the building is 450 feet tall with 34 floors.

Interesting Fact #3

WOAH!

Louisiana is the only state in the union that does not have counties. Its political subdivisions are called parishes.
